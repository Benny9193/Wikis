## Overview

**The Nuclear Option** is the final main quest in Fallout 4 for three of the four major factions: the Minutemen, Railroad, and Brotherhood of Steel. Each version involves destroying the Institute by detonating its nuclear reactor, though the approach and companions differ.

## Versions

### Minutemen Version
**Quest Giver:** Preston Garvey
**Trigger:** Institute hostile + Castle artillery ready

Requires:
- Becoming enemies with Institute
- Old Guns completed
- Artillery at the Castle
- Sturges provides Institute access

### Railroad Version
**Quest Giver:** Desdemona
**Trigger:** Completion of Burning Cover

Features:
- Freeing Institute Synths
- Patriot's assistance
- Z1-14 liberation
- Synth evacuation priority

### Brotherhood Version
**Quest Giver:** Elder Maxson
**Trigger:** Completion of Tactical Thinking

Includes:
- Liberty Prime deployment
- Direct military assault
- Maxson's personal involvement
- Brotherhood technology showcase

## Quest Objectives

All versions share core objectives:
1. Infiltrate the Institute
2. Reach the reactor
3. Plant fusion charges
4. Evacuate the Institute
5. Detonate the reactor

## Approach Differences

### Minutemen
- Use Brotherhood's stolen teleporter or sewer access
- Minimal military support
- Sturges assists remotely
- Settlers may help

### Railroad
- Focus on saving Synths
- Coordinate with Z1-14
- Desdemona's agents assist
- Data download possible

### Brotherhood
- Liberty Prime leads assault
- Massive military operation
- Power Armored soldiers support
- Most dramatic entrance

## Father's Fate

Father (Shaun) is dying regardless:
- Can speak with him one last time
- Can obtain synth Shaun from him
- Or can kill him
- His death is inevitable

## Synth Shaun

In all versions:
- Child synth Shaun can be saved
- Father may ask you to take him
- Becomes possible companion
- Lives at chosen settlement

## The Reactor

The Institute's reactor:
- Provides all their power
- Destruction causes massive explosion
- Fusion charges planted manually
- Timer gives escape window

## Escape

After planting charges:
- Emergency relay available
- Must escape before detonation
- Faction-specific extraction
- Dramatic escape sequence

## The Explosion

Visible from:
- C.I.T. ruins
- Surrounding Commonwealth
- Creates permanent crater
- Massive mushroom cloud

## Aftermath

Post-explosion:
- Institute is gone permanently
- Synth production ends
- Your faction controls the Commonwealth
- Faction-specific ending slides

## Faction-Specific Endings

### Minutemen
- Democracy returns to Commonwealth
- Settlements flourish
- Preston as advisor
- Peaceful rebuilding

### Railroad
- Synths freed and integrated
- Underground continues operating
- Focus on synth rights
- Freedom celebrated

### Brotherhood
- Military control established
- Technology secured
- Elder Maxson triumphant
- Order imposed

## Enemies Encountered

Inside the Institute:
- Synth soldiers (multiple generations)
- Turrets and security
- Institute scientists (non-hostile usually)
- Courser units

## Tips

- Bring power armor and heavy weapons
- Stock up on fusion cores (Brotherhood)
- Save before entering
- Explore for last-chance loot
- Speak to Father if desired

## What's Lost

Destroying the Institute means:
- No more Institute quests
- Synth production ends
- Advanced technology destroyed
- Scientists' fate uncertain

## Trivia

- The quest name references nuclear warfare
- Liberty Prime's return was highly anticipated
- The explosion is one of the largest in Fallout history
- Some Institute scientists can be warned (Railroad)
- The crater becomes a permanent map feature
- Father accepted his fate regardless of path
