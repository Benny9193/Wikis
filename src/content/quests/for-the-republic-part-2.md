## Overview

**For the Republic, Part 2** is the culmination of the NCR questline in Fallout: New Vegas. Working with Ambassador Crocker and Colonel Moore, the Courier secures alliances, eliminates threats, and leads the NCR to victory at the Second Battle of Hoover Dam.

## The NCR Path

The New California Republic represents:
- Democratic government
- Expansionist policies
- Military bureaucracy
- Taxation and law
- Civilian protection

## Quest Objectives

### Securing Alliances

#### The Boomers
- Complete Volare! questline
- Help raise the B-29 bomber
- Gain their air support
- Artillery barrage at Hoover Dam

#### Brotherhood of Steel
- Can broker peace (with effort)
- Or help Colonel Moore destroy them
- Peace requires convincing Elder McNamara
- Moore prefers elimination

#### The Great Khans
- Convince them to leave the Mojave
- Or turn them against Caesar
- Or eliminate them
- Multiple resolution options

#### The Kings
- Broker peace with NCR
- Resolve Freeside tensions
- "Kings' Gambit" questline
- Prevent conflict

### Eliminating Threats

#### Mr. House
- Must be dealt with eventually
- Access the Lucky 38 basement
- Disable or kill Mr. House
- Take control of Securitrons (or not)

#### The Legion
- Primary military enemy
- Weakened through various quests
- Final confrontation at Hoover Dam
- Caesar can be killed earlier

## The Second Battle of Hoover Dam

### Preparation
- All alliances should be secured
- Equipment and companions ready
- Fast travel may be disabled
- Save before proceeding

### The Battle
1. Report to General Oliver
2. Fight through Legion forces
3. Secure the dam's positions
4. Confront Legate Lanius
5. Determine the dam's fate

### Confronting Lanius
Multiple options:
- Kill him in combat (difficult)
- Speech check to make him withdraw
- Convince him the Legion will overextend
- Barter check to negotiate

### General Oliver's Reaction
After defeating the Legion:
- Oliver claims victory for NCR
- Courier can demand recognition
- Various endings based on choices
- NCR controls the dam

## Aftermath

The NCR ending results in:
- NCR controlling Hoover Dam
- Vegas becoming NCR territory
- Democracy imposed on Mojave
- Heavy taxation
- Military presence throughout

## Companion Impacts

Different companions react to NCR:
- **Boone** - Very supportive (ex-NCR)
- **Arcade** - Mixed (distrusts government)
- **Raul** - Neutral
- **Cass** - Generally supportive

## Rewards

- NCR fame (Idolized possible)
- Service rifle
- NCR Ranger combat armor (later)
- Caps rewards
- "Good" ending for NCR supporters

## Conflicts

This path opposes:
- Mr. House (must be eliminated)
- Caesar's Legion (primary enemy)
- Yes Man (alternative ending)
- Full independence for Vegas

## Tips

- Brotherhood peace is difficult but possible
- Colonel Moore is more ruthless than Crocker
- Speech is valuable for Lanius confrontation
- Secure all alliances before final battle

## Trivia

- The NCR ending is often considered "default"
- Colonel Moore is one of the more ruthless NCR officers
- General Oliver is unpopular among Rangers
- The dam provides power to the entire region
- NCR's bureaucracy is both strength and weakness
