## Overview

**The House Always Wins** is actually a series of quests (I through VIII) that form Mr. House's main questline in Fallout: New Vegas. By following this path, the Courier helps secure Mr. House's autocratic but technologically progressive vision for New Vegas's future.

## Mr. House

Robert Edwin House is:
- Pre-war billionaire CEO of RobCo
- Saved Las Vegas from nuclear destruction
- 261 years old, preserved in a life-support chamber
- Controls New Vegas via Securitron robots
- Seeks to rebuild civilization his way

## Quest Series

### The House Always Wins I
**Objective:** Deliver the Platinum Chip

After Ring-a-Ding-Ding, bring the chip to Mr. House:
- Enter the Lucky 38 (finally opened)
- Meet Mr. House on screen
- Learn his plans for the chip
- Receive access to Presidential Suite

### The House Always Wins II
**Objective:** Upgrade the Securitrons

Use the Platinum Chip at The Fort:
- Travel to Caesar's camp
- Access the underground bunker
- Upgrade the Securitron army
- Optional: Deal with Caesar

### The House Always Wins III
**Objective:** Handle the Omertas

Investigate the Omertas at Gomorrah:
- Discover their plot with Caesar
- Eliminate Nero and Big Sal
- Or convince them to stand down
- Prevent the attack on the Strip

### The House Always Wins IV
**Objective:** Secure the Brotherhood

Deal with the Brotherhood of Steel:
- House wants them destroyed
- Hidden Valley bunker must be handled
- Can negotiate with Elder McNamara
- Ultimately must eliminate them (for House)

### The House Always Wins V
**Objective:** Handle the Boomers

Secure Nellis Air Force Base alliance:
- Complete Volare! questline
- Help the Boomers raise the bomber
- Gain their support for the battle
- Their artillery aids in Hoover Dam

### The House Always Wins VI
**Objective:** The White Glove Society

Ensure the White Gloves' loyalty:
- Investigate the Ultra-Luxe
- Prevent return to cannibalism
- Or ensure Marjorie maintains control
- Secure their continued operation

### The House Always Wins VII
**Objective:** Prepare for Battle

Final preparations:
- Check on all faction statuses
- Ensure alliances are secured
- Prepare for Hoover Dam battle
- Receive final briefing from House

### The House Always Wins VIII
**Objective:** The Battle of Hoover Dam

The climactic battle:
- Fight alongside Securitrons
- Defeat Legion and NCR forces
- Secure the dam for Mr. House
- Activate the upgraded Securitron army

## Mr. House's Vision

If successful, Mr. House plans:
- Autocratic control of Vegas
- Technological advancement
- Space colonization within decades
- "Enlightened despotism"
- Minimal individual freedom

## Rewards

Throughout the questline:
- Caps for each completed quest
- Lucky 38 Presidential Suite
- Securitron support in combat
- High-tech ending for Vegas

## Conflicts

This path conflicts with:
- **NCR** - Must be pushed out
- **Legion** - Must be destroyed
- **Brotherhood** - Must be eliminated
- **Yes Man** - Alternative independent path

## Key Locations

- Lucky 38 Casino (headquarters)
- The Fort (Securitron vault)
- Gomorrah (Omertas)
- Hidden Valley (Brotherhood)
- Nellis AFB (Boomers)
- Hoover Dam (finale)

## Tips

- Mr. House pays well for missions
- Keep the Brotherhood alive as long as possible
- Completing faction quests helps before declaring allegiance
- The Securitron army is formidable at Hoover Dam

## Ending

The House ending features:
- Mr. House in control of Vegas
- NCR retreating from Mojave
- Legion crushed at the dam
- Technological progress promised
- Questions about freedom vs. progress
