## Overview

**Ring-a-Ding-Ding!** is a pivotal main quest in Fallout: New Vegas. After tracking Benny across the Mojave, the Courier finally confronts the man who shot them in the head and left them for dead in the Goodsprings cemetery.

## Quest Objectives

1. Enter the Strip
2. Go to The Tops casino
3. Confront Benny
4. Recover the Platinum Chip

## Background

### The Setup
Benny, chairman of The Tops casino, intercepted the Courier and stole the Platinum Chipâ€”a valuable item being delivered to Mr. House. He shot the Courier and buried them in Goodsprings Cemetery, but the Courier survived thanks to Victor and Doc Mitchell.

### The Chase
Through "Ain't That a Kick in the Head" and "They Went That-a-Way," the Courier tracked Benny's path from Goodsprings through Primm, Nipton, Novac, Boulder City, and finally to the Strip.

## Entering the Strip

### Credit Check
The Strip requires 2,000 caps or a passport:
- Science check on the Securitron
- Bribe the gate guard
- Obtain a passport (King's Gambit quest)
- Have enough caps on hand

### The Tops Security
Upon entering The Tops:
- All weapons are confiscated
- Holdout weapons can be smuggled in
- Sneak skill determines what you can keep
- Black Widow perk allows special approach

## Confronting Benny

Multiple approaches exist:

### Direct Confrontation
- Speak with Benny in the casino
- He attempts to talk his way out
- Can attack him directly
- His bodyguards will defend him

### Stealth Approach
- Sneak to his suite (13th floor)
- Access through maintenance areas
- Catch him unprepared
- Avoid casino security

### Black Widow/Confirmed Bachelor
- Seduce Benny (female Courier)
- Invite him to his suite
- Kill him in his sleep
- Easiest and most ironic approach

### Let Him Go
- Accept his explanations
- Allow him to leave
- He escapes to The Fort
- Confrontation delayed to later

## Benny's Suite

Regardless of approach, search his suite:
- Find Yes Man (disabled Securitron)
- Discover Benny's plans
- Learn about the Platinum Chip
- Opens Independent Vegas path

## Yes Man

A crucial discovery in Benny's suite:
- Reprogrammed Securitron
- Cannot refuse requests
- Explains Benny's plan
- Offers alternative to all factions

## The Platinum Chip

Recovery of the chip allows:
- Delivery to Mr. House
- Use for independent Vegas
- Give to NCR or Legion
- Opens all endgame paths

## Aftermath

### If Benny Killed
- The Tops continues operating
- His bodyguards become neutral
- Maria (his gun) can be looted
- Quest completes normally

### If Benny Escaped
- He flees to Caesar's Legion
- "Render Unto Caesar" continues pursuit
- Can be killed at The Fort
- The chip must still be recovered

## Related Quests

This quest opens major faction questlines:
- **The House Always Wins** - Mr. House path
- **Wild Card** - Independent path (via Yes Man)
- **Render Unto Caesar** - Legion path
- **Things That Go Boom** - NCR path eventually

## Tips

- Bring holdout weapons (small, concealable)
- Save before entering The Tops
- Explore all dialogue options
- Don't miss Yes Man in the suite
- Consider letting Benny escape for more options

## Key Items

- **Platinum Chip** - Central MacGuffin of the game
- **Maria** - Benny's unique 9mm pistol
- **Benny's Suit** - Stylish checked blazer

## Trivia

- "Ring-a-Ding-Ding" is a Dean Martin reference
- Benny is voiced by Matthew Perry (Friends)
- The Black Widow option is a fan-favorite
- Yes Man was added to guarantee a completable game
- The Platinum Chip is actually a data storage device
