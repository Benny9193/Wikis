## Overview

**War Never Changes** is the prologue quest of Fallout 4, one of the most cinematic openings in the series. It covers the moments before the Great War, the nuclear devastation, and the Sole Survivor's 210-year cryogenic sleep in Vault 111.

## Quest Objectives

1. Create your character
2. Experience the morning of October 23, 2077
3. Flee to Vault 111
4. Enter cryogenic suspension
5. Witness the kidnapping of Shaun
6. Escape Vault 111
7. Return home

## Pre-War Sequence

### Character Creation
The quest begins at the bathroom mirror:
- Customize appearance (either spouse)
- Choose name
- Spouse adjusts to match your choices
- Codsworth asks for name input

### Morning Routine
Experience normal pre-war life:
- Interact with spouse and baby Shaun
- Meet Codsworth the robot butler
- Answer the door for a Vault-Tec representative
- Confirm vault registration

### The Vault-Tec Rep
A salesman confirms your vault spot:
- Provides backstory on Vault-Tec
- Assigns you to Vault 111
- Hints at the coming apocalypse
- One of few characters to survive

## The Great War

### The Emergency
While watching TV, the news reports:
- Nuclear detonations confirmed
- Philadelphia, New York hit
- Imminent attack on Boston
- Sirens begin blaring

### Race to the Vault
Flee to Vault 111:
- Sprint through Sanctuary Hills
- Chaos as neighbors panic
- Military guards at the vault
- Verify identity at checkpoint

### Nuclear Detonation
On the vault elevator:
- Witness the nuclear blast
- Shockwave approaches
- Elevator descends just in time
- Pre-war world ends

## Vault 111

### Cryogenic Suspension
Inside Vault 111:
- Directed to "decontamination pods"
- Actually cryogenic chambers
- Frozen with other residents
- Time passes unknowingly

### The Kidnapping
Awakened briefly:
- Two figures open pods
- Your spouse is murdered
- Shaun is taken
- Refrozen before you can act

### Kellogg
The kidnapper is Conrad Kellogg:
- Institute mercenary
- Important later antagonist
- Working for "the old man"
- Face visible through glass

## Awakening

### 210 Years Later
The pod malfunctions:
- Released from cryogenic sleep
- All other residents dead
- Spouse's body remains
- Wedding ring can be taken

### Escaping the Vault
Navigate the facility:
- Pick up the Pip-Boy
- Find security baton
- Fight radroaches
- Unlock the vault door

## Return to Sanctuary

### Changed World
Emerge to devastation:
- Sanctuary Hills destroyed
- 210 years have passed
- Codsworth still functional
- First companion available

### Codsworth's Information
The robot reveals:
- He's been waiting 210 years
- Tried to keep the house tidy
- Knows nothing of Shaun
- Suggests investigating Concord

## Completion

The quest ends when you:
- Exit Vault 111
- Speak with Codsworth
- Triggers "Out of Time"

## Key Items Obtained

- **Pip-Boy 3000 Mark IV** - Essential equipment
- **10mm Pistol** - First firearm
- **Vault 111 Jumpsuit** - Iconic outfit
- **Security Baton** - First melee weapon
- **Wedding Ring** - From spouse (optional)

## Themes

The quest establishes:
- The Sole Survivor's motivation (find Shaun)
- The pre-war/post-war contrast
- Personal stakes in the story
- The mystery of the Institute

## Tips

- Explore Vault 111 thoroughly
- The Cryolator in the overseer's office requires Master lockpicking
- Codsworth can be a companion immediately
- Return to Vault 111 for Curie later

## Trivia

- Ron Perlman narrates "War never changes"
- The pre-war segment is unique in the series
- Your spouse's name is Nate or Nora
- The exact date is October 23, 2077
- The morning TV shows escalating news
- Vault 111's experiment was cryogenic preservation
