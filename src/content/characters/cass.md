## Overview

**Rose of Sharon Cassidy**, commonly known as **Cass**, is the owner of the defunct Cassidy Caravans and a potential companion in Fallout: New Vegas. She's a hard-drinking, straight-talking caravan merchant with a sharp tongue and strong moral compass.

## Background

### Family Legacy

Cass is the daughter of John Cassidy, a companion from Fallout 2:
- Inherited her father's heart condition
- Also inherited his sharp wit
- Built her own caravan business
- Carries on the Cassidy name

### Cassidy Caravans

Cass built a successful caravan business:
- Ran routes throughout the Mojave
- Employed several workers
- Had contracts with various settlements
- The caravan was mysteriously destroyed

## First Encounter

Found at Mojave Outpost:
- Drowning her sorrows at the bar
- Stranded due to caravan destruction
- Waiting for NCR travel clearance
- Initially cannot leave the area

## Recruitment

Recruiting Cass requires:
1. Complete "Heartache by the Number" to a certain point
2. Convince her to sell Cassidy Caravans to the Crimson Caravan
3. Or help her investigate the caravan's destruction
4. She joins after resolving her business

## Personal Quest

**Heartache by the Number** involves:
- Investigating who destroyed her caravan
- Discovering a conspiracy between Van Graffs and Crimson Caravan
- Choosing justice (NCR) or revenge (killing those responsible)
- Resolution affects her ending

## Combat Abilities

### Fighting Style
- Prefers shotguns and revolvers
- Good with close-to-mid range weapons
- Moderate combat effectiveness
- Tough survivor mentality

### Default Equipment
- Leather armor
- Caravan shotgun
- Suitable for wasteland travel

## Companion Perk

**Whiskey Rose** - While Cass is a companion, the player gains increased Damage Threshold when drinking whiskey, and alcohol addiction is negated.

## Affinity

### Likes
- Being honest and direct
- Standing up to bullies
- Drinking alcohol
- Fair business dealings
- NCR generally

### Dislikes
- Working with Legion
- Theft and stealing
- Unnecessary cruelty
- Betraying trust

### Special Reactions
- Comments on locations with whiskey
- Discusses caravan life
- Shares stories about her father

## Views on Factions

- **NCR** - Generally supportive despite bureaucratic frustrations
- **Legion** - Despises their treatment of women
- **Van Graffs** - Hatred (they destroyed her caravan)
- **Crimson Caravan** - Complicated relationship

## Personality

Cass is defined by:
- Brutal honesty
- Heavy drinking
- Tough exterior, caring heart
- Pride in her work
- Loyalty to friends

## Romance

Cass is not a romance option in the traditional sense, but:
- Flirtatious dialogue exists
- Implications of off-screen encounters
- One-night stand possibility mentioned

## Heart Condition

Like her father:
- Cannot use chems safely
- Drinking is her vice instead
- Referenced in dialogue
- Genetic inheritance

## Endings

Cass's ending depends on:
- Resolution of her personal quest

## Trivia

- "Rose of Sharon" is a biblical reference
- Her father appeared in Fallout 2
- One of the more adult-oriented companions
- Whiskey Rose perk encourages roleplay drinking
- She's one of few companions critical of Legion openly
- Her caravan was named after her father's legacy
