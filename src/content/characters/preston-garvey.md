## Overview

**Preston Garvey** is a member of the Commonwealth Minutemen and one of the first companions encountered in Fallout 4. He serves as the player's primary contact for the Minutemen faction and is known for frequently assigning settlement defense quests.

## Background

Preston was born in the Commonwealth and joined the Minutemen as a young man, believing in their mission to protect settlers. He rose to the rank of Colonel before the Minutemen's near-destruction at Quincy.

### The Quincy Massacre

Preston was present at Quincy when the Gunners attacked and Minutemen Colonel Hollis was killed by the traitor Clint. Preston led a group of survivors on a desperate retreat that eventually brought them to Concord.

## First Encounter

The player meets Preston and his group at the Museum of Freedom in Concord, where they are trapped by raiders and a Deathclaw. After rescuing them, Preston invites the player to Sanctuary Hills and offers membership in the Minutemen.

## Role in the Minutemen

As the player rebuilds the Minutemen, Preston serves as:

- **Quest giver** - Assigns settlement defense and recruitment missions
- **Advisor** - Provides guidance on Minutemen activities
- **Companion** - Available as a traveling partner

## Companion Perk

**United We Stand** - When at maximum affinity with Preston, gain +20% damage and +20 damage resistance when facing three or more enemies.

## Affinity

Preston approves of:
- Helping settlers and communities
- Generous and kind actions
- Building up settlements
- Joining the Minutemen

Preston disapproves of:
- Selfish or cruel behavior
- Harming innocents
- Demanding payment for help
- Working with raiders
