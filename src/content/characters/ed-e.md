## Overview

**ED-E** (Eyebot Duraframe Subject E) is a heavily modified Eyebot found in Primm. Originally an Enclave reconnaissance robot, ED-E traveled thousands of miles from the East Coast before breaking down in the Mojave. When repaired, he becomes a loyal and useful companion.

## Background

### Enclave Origins

ED-E was created at Adams Air Force Base:
- Part of the Enclave's Eyebot program
- Duraframe model (enhanced durability)
- Designed for reconnaissance
- Modified with experimental upgrades

### Doctor Whitley

ED-E's creator was Dr. Whitley:
- Enclave scientist at Adams AFB
- Developed strong attachment to ED-E
- Recorded audio logs in ED-E's memory
- Sent ED-E west for safety

### Journey West

ED-E traveled across America:
- Started at Adams AFB (Capital Wasteland)
- Programmed destination: Navarro
- Journey took years
- Damaged multiple times
- Finally broke down in Primm

## First Encounter

Found in the Mojave Express office in Primm:
- Severely damaged
- Requires repair to activate
- Repair skill 65, or
- Science skill 55 with scrap parts
- Or specific components

## Recruitment

After repair:
- ED-E reactivates
- Becomes available as companion
- Counts as non-humanoid slot
- Can accompany humanoid companions

## Combat Abilities

### Weapons
- Built-in laser weapon
- Electrical attack
- Decent damage output
- Automatic targeting

### Utility
- Enhanced sensors (detects enemies)
- Provides radar coverage
- Marks enemies on compass
- Can carry items

## Companion Perk

**Enhanced Sensors** - While ED-E is a companion, the player gains increased detection of enemies at longer range and can detect enemies even while sneaking.

## Communication

ED-E cannot speak:
- Communicates through beeps
- Musical tones convey emotion
- Players interpret meaning
- Surprisingly expressive

## Audio Logs

ED-E contains recordings:
- Dr. Whitley's logs
- Triggered at specific locations
- Reveal ED-E's origin story
- Unlock through exploration

### Trigger Locations
- Helios One
- REPCONN headquarters
- Brotherhood bunker
- Other technology sites

## Upgrades

ED-E can be upgraded:
- Through Lonesome Road DLC
- By specific factions in base game
- Choice affects abilities
- Permanent modifications

### Lonesome Road

The DLC features ED-E prominently:
- A copy of ED-E accompanies player
- More backstory revealed
- Multiple upgrade options
- Enhanced emotional connection

## Personality

Despite lacking voice:
- Clearly shows loyalty
- Reacts to situations
- Protective of the Courier
- Enthusiastic about helping
- Curious about the world

## Views on Factions

ED-E has special interactions with:
- **Brotherhood** - Recognizes tech, may trigger logs
- **Enclave remnants** - Origin connection
- **NCR** - Neutral

## Quotes (Translations)

ED-E's beeps often mean:
- Happy chirps when helping
- Warning tones for danger
- Sad sounds when damaged
- Excited beeps at technology

## Trivia

- Name pronounced "Eddie"
- One of only two non-humanoid companions
- Can be combined with any human companion
- Lonesome Road explores his story deeply
- Dr. Whitley clearly cared about him
- The journey from DC to Nevada is roughly 2,400 miles
- Popular companion due to utility and personality
- Players often become emotionally attached despite his silence
