## Overview

**Piper Wright** is an investigative journalist who runs the newspaper Publick Occurrences in Diamond City. She is one of the first companions encountered in Fallout 4 and is known for her relentless pursuit of the truth, particularly regarding the Institute.

## Background

Piper grew up in a small settlement with her father, a member of the local militia, and her younger sister Nat. After her father was murdered by raiders who had been tipped off by a corrupt militia captain, Piper dedicated herself to exposing corruption and injustice.

### Arriving in Diamond City

Piper moved to Diamond City to pursue journalism. She founded Publick Occurrences, Diamond City's only newspaper, which she runs out of a small building near the entrance with help from her sister Nat.

## First Encounter

The player meets Piper at the Diamond City gate, where she's been locked out by Mayor McDonough after publishing an article accusing him of being a synth. She convinces the player to help her bluff her way back inside.

## Publick Occurrences

Piper's newspaper covers:
- Institute activities and synth sightings
- Political corruption
- Wasteland news and dangers
- Human interest stories

Her aggressive journalism has made her enemies among Diamond City's elite but earned her respect from common citizens.

## Companion Abilities

### Combat Style
- Prefers ranged weapons
- Good with pistols and rifles
- Moderate combat effectiveness
- Will use any weapon given to her

### Special Ability
Piper can pick locks when asked, useful for accessing secured areas.

## Companion Perk

**Gift of Gab** - When at maximum affinity with Piper, the player gains double XP for discovering locations and passing Speech challenges.

## Affinity

### Likes
- Helping others selflessly
- Investigating and uncovering secrets
- Picking locks (non-owned)
- Being generous and kind
- Supporting the downtrodden

### Dislikes
- Cruelty and violence against innocents
- Greed and selfishness
- Working with the Institute
- Lying and deception

### Loves
- Donating items to the poor
- Exposing corruption

## Romance

Piper is available as a romance option:
- Requires maximum affinity
- Must complete her personal quest
- Flirt options become available
- Unlocks romance dialogue and perks

## Personal Quest

**Story of the Century** - Piper asks the player to help her write the story of their search for Shaun. Completing this quest and reaching maximum affinity unlocks her perk.

## Relationship with Nat

Piper is fiercely protective of her younger sister Natalie:
- Nat helps sell newspapers
- Piper worries about her safety
- Their bond is central to Piper's character
- Nat looks up to her sister

## Views on Factions

- **Institute** - Deep distrust, primary investigative target
- **Brotherhood** - Skeptical of military authority
- **Railroad** - Sympathetic to their cause
- **Minutemen** - Supportive of helping settlements
