## Overview

**Curie** (Contagions Vulnerability Robotic Infirmary Engineer) is a Miss Nanny robot encountered in Vault 81. Originally programmed as a medical assistant, she developed sentience over 200 years of isolation and can later transfer her consciousness into a synth body.

## Background

### Vault 81 Origins

Curie was stationed in Vault 81's secret research wing:
- Part of the vault's hidden medical research
- Worked alongside human scientists
- Researchers eventually died of old age
- Curie remained alone for nearly two centuries

### Scientific Development

During her isolation, Curie:
- Completed research on a universal cure
- Developed far beyond her programming
- Became fully sentient
- Yearned to contribute to the world

## First Encounter

Found during "Hole in the Wall":
- Vault 81 has a secret section
- Austin contracts mole rat disease
- Curie has developed a cure
- She gives up her only sample to save him

## Recruitment

After completing "Hole in the Wall":
- Curie asks to see the outside world
- Wants to continue her research
- Joins as a companion
- Grateful for her freedom

## Transformation Quest

**Emergent Behavior** is Curie's personal quest:
- She struggles with robot limitations
- Cannot truly feel or experience
- Desires a human (synth) body
- Requires Railroad assistance

### Becoming a Synth

Doctor Amari at the Memory Den can help:
- Transfer consciousness to synth body
- Gains human appearance
- Retains all memories and personality
- Opens romance option

## Combat Abilities

### As Robot
- Built-in laser weapon
- Moderate combat effectiveness
- Can use medical functions
- Durable construction

### As Synth
- Uses conventional weapons
- Similar to human companions
- Can wear armor
- Full companion functionality

## Companion Perk

**Combat Medic** - When at maximum affinity with Curie, once per day when health drops below 10%, you heal 100 HP.

## Personality

Curie is characterized by:

- French accent and mannerisms

- Scientific enthusiasm

## Scientific Knowledge

Curie's expertise includes:

- Chemistry

## Views on Synths

After transformation:

- Explores what it means to be human
