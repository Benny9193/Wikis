## Overview

**Fallout** is the original game in the Fallout series, developed and published by Interplay Entertainment in 1997. It established the post-apocalyptic setting, dark humor, and RPG mechanics that would define the franchise.

## Setting

The game is set in 2161, 84 years after the Great War that devastated the world. The player begins in Vault 13, located in Southern California.

## Plot

The player takes on the role of the Vault Dweller, tasked with finding a replacement Water Chip to save Vault 13's failing water recycling system. The quest leads through the California wasteland and eventually reveals a much greater threatâ€”the Super Mutant army led by the Master.

### Main Objectives

1. Find a replacement Water Chip (150-day time limit initially)
2. Destroy the Super Mutant threat
3. Stop the Master's plan to convert humanity

## Gameplay

### SPECIAL System

Fallout introduced the SPECIAL attribute system:

- **S**trength
- **P**erception
- **E**ndurance
- **C**harisma
- **I**ntelligence
- **A**gility
- **L**uck

### Turn-Based Combat

Combat uses Action Points in a turn-based system. Players can target specific body parts for different effects.

### Skills and Perks

The game features numerous skills (Small Guns, Speech, Lockpick, etc.) and perks gained every few levels that provide special abilities.

## Major Locations

- **Vault 13** - The player's home vault
- **Shady Sands** - A small settlement, future capital of the NCR
- **Junktown** - Trading hub with morally complex quests
- **The Hub** - Major trading center of the wasteland
- **Necropolis** - City of ghouls
- **The Boneyard** - Ruins of Los Angeles
- **Mariposa Military Base** - Source of the FEV and Super Mutants
- **The Cathedral** - The Master's headquarters

## Key Characters

- **The Vault Dweller** - Player character
- **The Master** - Main antagonist, mutant leader
- **The Overseer** - Leader of Vault 13
- **Harold** - Ghoul who appears in multiple games
- **Dogmeat** - Iconic canine companion
- **Ian** - First recruitable companion

## Legacy

Fallout established:

- The retro-futuristic 1950s aesthetic
- Dark humor in a serious setting
- Multiple solutions to quests
- Moral ambiguity and consequences
- The SPECIAL system still used today

## Reception

The game received critical acclaim for its open-ended gameplay, writing, and atmosphere. It won numerous awards and is considered one of the greatest RPGs ever made.

## Trivia

- Originally titled "Vault 13: A GURPS Post-Nuclear Adventure"
- The game engine was later used for Fallout 2
- Can be completed in under 10 minutes with speedrun tactics
- Multiple endings based on player choices
- The time limit was controversial and partially removed in patches
