## Overview

**Fallout: New Vegas** is a post-apocalyptic action role-playing game developed by Obsidian Entertainment and published by Bethesda Softworks. Released in October 2010, it is considered by many fans to be one of the finest entries in the Fallout series.

## Setting

The game takes place in the year 2281, four years after the events of Fallout 3, in the Mojave Wasteland, a region covering parts of the former states of Nevada, California, and Arizona. The central location is the city of New Vegas, the post-apocalyptic version of Las Vegas.

## Plot

The player takes on the role of the Courier, a delivery person who is shot and left for dead in the Mojave Desert. After being rescued by a robot named Victor and nursed back to health by Doc Mitchell in Goodsprings, the Courier sets out to find their attempted murderer and recover the mysterious package they were carrying.

## Major Factions

The game features several major factions vying for control of the Mojave:

- **New California Republic (NCR)** - A democratic federation seeking to expand into the Mojave
- **Caesar's Legion** - A slavers' army based on ancient Roman society
- **Mr. House** - The mysterious ruler of New Vegas
- **Yes Man** - An AI that enables an independent Vegas ending

## Reception

Fallout: New Vegas received critical acclaim for its writing, quest design, faction system, and role-playing depth. Despite technical issues at launch, it has developed a devoted fanbase and is often cited as the best modern Fallout game.
