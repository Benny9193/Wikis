## Overview

**Fallout 2** is the second game in the Fallout series, developed by Black Isle Studios and published by Interplay Entertainment in 1998. It expanded on the original's gameplay while introducing more humor, pop culture references, and a larger game world.

## Setting

The game is set in 2241, 80 years after the events of Fallout 1. The world has developed further, with new nations and settlements emerging from the wasteland.

## Plot

The player is the Chosen One, a descendant of the original Vault Dweller. Their tribal village of Arroyo is suffering from drought and crop failure. The Chosen One must find the Garden of Eden Creation Kit (G.E.C.K.) to save their people.

### Main Objectives

1. Find the G.E.C.K. to save Arroyo
2. Uncover the Enclave's sinister plans
3. Rescue the kidnapped villagers
4. Defeat the Enclave

## Gameplay Improvements

### Expanded Features

- Larger game world with more locations
- More companions with better AI
- Car transportation (Highwayman)
- Expanded dialogue and quest options
- More weapons and armor

### New Mechanics

- Reputation system with individual towns
- More detailed companion control
- Gambling and boxing minigames
- Marriage option

## Major Locations

- **Arroyo** - Tribal village, starting location
- **Klamath** - First settlement encountered
- **The Den** - Lawless slaver town
- **Vault City** - Isolationist vault-based city
- **Gecko** - Ghoul settlement with power plant
- **New Reno** - City controlled by crime families
- **Redding** - Mining town
- **Broken Hills** - Mutant-human integrated town
- **NCR** - Capital of the New California Republic
- **San Francisco** - Hub of the Shi and Hubologists
- **Navarro** - Enclave outpost
- **Control Station Enclave** - Oil rig, final location

## Key Characters

- **The Chosen One** - Player character
- **President Richardson** - Enclave leader
- **Frank Horrigan** - Enclave super soldier, final boss
- **Marcus** - Super Mutant sheriff
- **Cassidy** - Companion, father of Cass from New Vegas
- **Myron** - Creator of Jet
- **Sulik** - Tribal warrior companion
- **Vic** - Trader companion

## New California Republic

Fallout 2 shows the NCR as an established nation:

- Democratic government
- Standing army
- Currency (NCR dollars)
- Expanding territory
- Shady Sands as capital

## Legacy

The game expanded on the original's foundation:

- Deeper faction relationships
- More morally gray choices
- Established the Enclave as antagonists
- Showed civilization rebuilding
- More Easter eggs and humor

## Reception

Fallout 2 received critical acclaim, praised for its expanded world and storytelling. Some criticized its bugs at launch and occasionally excessive humor.

## Trivia

- Contains numerous pop culture references and Easter eggs
- The car was a major addition for traversing the larger map
- New Reno's crime families were inspired by Godfather films
- Can become a porn star, boxer, or made man
- Features a talking plant named Seymour
