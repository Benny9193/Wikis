## Overview

**Fallout 4** is an action role-playing game developed by Bethesda Game Studios and published by Bethesda Softworks. Released in November 2015, it introduced significant gameplay changes including a voiced protagonist, revamped combat, and an extensive crafting system.

## Setting

The game is set in the year 2287, ten years after Fallout 3, in the region known as the Commonwealthâ€”the post-apocalyptic ruins of Boston, Massachusetts, and surrounding areas.

## Plot

The player character, known as the Sole Survivor, emerges from Vault 111 after being cryogenically frozen for 210 years. They witness the murder of their spouse and kidnapping of their infant son, Shaun. The main quest involves tracking down the mysterious Institute and finding Shaun.

## Major Factions

- **The Minutemen** - A volunteer militia seeking to protect Commonwealth settlements
- **The Brotherhood of Steel** - A militaristic organization seeking to control advanced technology
- **The Railroad** - A secretive group dedicated to freeing synthetic humans
- **The Institute** - An advanced scientific organization hidden beneath MIT

## Key Features

### Settlement Building
Players can construct and manage settlements throughout the Commonwealth, building structures, defenses, and amenities for settlers.

### Crafting System
Extensive weapon and armor modification system allowing players to customize their equipment.

### Companions
Various companions can join the player, each with unique personalities, quests, and perks.
