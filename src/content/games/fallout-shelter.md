## Overview

**Fallout Shelter** is a free-to-play simulation game developed by Bethesda Game Studios. Released alongside the Fallout 4 announcement in 2015, it became a surprise hit, allowing players to build and manage their own vault as an Overseer.

## Gameplay

### Vault Management

Players build underground vaults by:
- Constructing rooms for different purposes
- Assigning dwellers to appropriate jobs
- Managing resources (power, food, water)
- Defending against threats
- Expanding deeper underground

### Room Types

#### Resource Rooms
- **Power Generator/Nuclear Reactor** - Electricity
- **Diner/Garden** - Food production
- **Water Treatment/Purification** - Water supply

#### Production Rooms
- **Medbay/Science Lab** - Stimpaks and RadAway
- **Weapons/Outfit Workshop** - Crafting equipment

#### Training Rooms
- **Weight Room** - Strength
- **Armory** - Perception
- **Fitness Room** - Endurance
- **Lounge** - Charisma
- **Classroom** - Intelligence
- **Athletics Room** - Agility
- **Game Room** - Luck

#### Special Rooms
- **Living Quarters** - Population capacity
- **Radio Room** - Attract new dwellers
- **Storage** - Item capacity
- **Overseer's Office** - Quest management

### SPECIAL System

Dwellers have SPECIAL stats affecting their work:
- **Strength** - Power production
- **Perception** - Water production
- **Endurance** - Exploration survival
- **Charisma** - Radio effectiveness, breeding
- **Intelligence** - Medical production
- **Agility** - Food production
- **Luck** - Rush success, caps

## Dwellers

### Obtaining Dwellers
- Natural births in living quarters
- Radio room recruitment
- Lunchbox rewards
- Quest rewards
- Wasteland encounters

### Legendary Dwellers
Special characters from the Fallout series:
- Preston Garvey
- Piper Wright
- Three Dog
- Sarah Lyons
- Many others

## Exploration

### Wasteland Exploration
Send dwellers into the wasteland to:
- Find weapons and outfits
- Collect caps
- Encounter enemies
- Discover locations

### Quests
Structured missions in specific locations:
- Combat encounters
- Boss fights
- Unique rewards
- Story content

## Threats

### Raiders
Attack the vault entrance periodically.

### Radroaches/Mole Rats
Spawn in rooms, especially during failed rushes.

### Fires
Can break out in rooms.

### Deathclaws
Powerful enemies that appear in larger vaults.

## Monetization

Free-to-play with optional purchases:
- **Lunchboxes** - Random rewards including legendary items
- **Pet Carriers** - Bonus-granting pets
- **Nuka-Cola Quantum** - Speed up actions
- **Starter Packs** - Bundles for new players

The game is playable without spending money.

## Updates

Major additions over time:
- Crafting system
- Quests and exploration locations
- Pets
- Themes and decorations
- Holiday events

## Reception

Fallout Shelter received positive reviews:
- Praised for accessibility
- Engaging core loop
- Fair free-to-play model
- Good Fallout atmosphere
- Over 100 million downloads

## Spin-off

**Fallout Shelter Online** - An expanded version released in Asia with more RPG elements and multiplayer features.
