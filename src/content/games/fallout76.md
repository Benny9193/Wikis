## Overview

**Fallout 76** is an online action role-playing game developed by Bethesda Game Studios. Released in 2018, it is the first multiplayer entry in the Fallout series, allowing players to explore the wasteland together in a shared world.

## Setting

Set in 2102, just 25 years after the Great War, Fallout 76 is the earliest game chronologically in the series. The setting is Appalachiaâ€”the region encompassing West Virginia and surrounding areas.

## Plot

Players are residents of Vault 76, a control vault designed to open 20 years after the war. On Reclamation Day, dwellers emerge to rebuild America. The main storyline involves tracking Vault 76's Overseer and uncovering the mystery of the Scorched Plague threatening the region.

### Major Story Arcs

- **Original Main Quest** - Follow the Overseer's journey
- **Wastelanders** - Human NPCs return to Appalachia
- **Steel Dawn/Steel Reign** - Brotherhood of Steel arrives
- **Expeditions: The Pitt** - Return to Pittsburgh
- **Skyline Valley** - Lost vault and storm mysteries

## Gameplay

### Multiplayer

- Shared world with other players
- Public events and group content
- PvP options (limited)
- Private worlds available

### C.A.M.P. System

Players can build personal camps anywhere in the world:
- Portable base building
- Crafting stations
- Vendor capabilities
- Fast travel points

### Seasons and Events

Regular content updates include:
- Seasonal scoreboards with rewards
- Limited-time events
- New quests and locations
- Quality of life improvements

## Major Factions

- **Settlers** - Arrived with Wastelanders, based at Foundation
- **Raiders** - Returned raiders at Crater
- **Brotherhood of Steel** - Expeditionary force from California
- **Responders** - Fallen first responders organization
- **Free States** - Pre-war survivalist group
- **Enclave** - Automated remnants at Whitespring

## Major Locations

- **Vault 76** - Starting location
- **Flatwoods** - Early game town
- **The Wayward** - Bar and quest hub
- **Foundation** - Settler headquarters
- **Crater** - Raider headquarters
- **Fort Atlas** - Brotherhood base
- **Whitespring Resort** - Major landmark and Enclave bunker
- **Watoga** - Automated city
- **The Cranberry Bog** - High-level endgame zone

## Enemies

### The Scorched

The primary threat unique to Fallout 76:
- Humans infected by Scorched Plague
- Controlled by Scorchbeasts
- Spread the infection to others

### Scorchbeasts

Massive bat-like creatures that spread the plague. The Scorchbeast Queen is a major endgame boss.

### Cryptids

West Virginia folklore creatures:
- Mothman
- Flatwoods Monster
- Grafton Monster
- Wendigo
- Snallygaster

## Evolution

The game has significantly evolved since launch:

### Launch Issues (2018)
- Lack of human NPCs
- Technical problems
- Limited endgame content

### Major Updates
- **Wastelanders (2020)** - Added human NPCs and dialogue
- **One Wasteland (2020)** - Level scaling
- **Steel Dawn (2020)** - Brotherhood storyline
- **Expeditions (2022)** - Instanced missions outside Appalachia
- **Atlantic City (2024)** - New major region

## Subscription Service

Fallout 1st offers:
- Private worlds
- Unlimited scrap storage
- Monthly Atoms currency
- Exclusive cosmetics
- Survival tent

## Reception

Initially criticized for bugs and lack of NPCs, the game has improved significantly through updates. The Wastelanders expansion was particularly well-received for adding traditional Fallout elements.

## Trivia

- First Fallout without a definitive ending
- "Country Roads" by John Denver featured in marketing
- Originally no human NPCs at all
- West Virginia setting inspired by developer vacations
