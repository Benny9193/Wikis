## Overview

**Goodsprings** is a small frontier settlement in the southern Mojave Wasteland. It serves as the starting location for Fallout: New Vegas, where the Courier recovers from being shot in the head and left for dead.

## History

Goodsprings is based on the real town of the same name in Nevada. In the Fallout universe, it has survived as a small ranching and farming community, maintaining relative independence from major factions.

## The Courier's Recovery

The game begins with Doc Mitchell reviving the Courier in his home after Victor the Securitron dug them out of a shallow grave at the nearby Goodsprings Cemetery.

### Tutorial

Goodsprings serves as the tutorial area, teaching players:
- Basic movement and interaction
- Character creation (via Doc Mitchell's psychological evaluation)
- Combat basics
- Dialogue and faction mechanics

## Layout

### Key Locations

- **Doc Mitchell's House** - Starting location, medical services
- **Goodsprings General Store** - Supplies and ammunition
- **Prospector Saloon** - Bar, information, quest hub
- **Goodsprings Schoolhouse** - Abandoned, minor loot
- **Goodsprings Source** - Water supply
- **Goodsprings Cemetery** - Where the Courier was buried

## Key Characters

- **Doc Mitchell** - Doctor who saves the Courier's life
- **Sunny Smiles** - Teaches combat basics, caravan guard
- **Trudy** - Saloon owner, community leader
- **Chet** - General store owner
- **Easy Pete** - Retired prospector with dynamite expertise
- **Victor** - Mysterious Securitron who rescued the Courier

## The Powder Gangers Conflict

The main quest choice in Goodsprings:

### Ghost Town Gunfight
- Side with Goodsprings against the Powder Gangers
- Help defend the town from Joe Cobb's gang
- Positive reputation with Goodsprings
- Negative reputation with Powder Gangers

### Run Goodsprings Run
- Side with Joe Cobb and the Powder Gangers
- Help attack and subjugate Goodsprings
- Positive reputation with Powder Gangers
- Negative reputation with Goodsprings

## Economy

- Small-scale farming (bighorners)
- Trading post along caravan routes
- Limited resources and population

## Strategic Importance

Despite its small size, Goodsprings offers:
- Safe respite for travelers
- Medical care from Doc Mitchell
- Basic supplies
- Neutral ground away from major faction conflicts

## Trivia

- Named after the real Goodsprings, Nevada
- The Pioneer Saloon (real location) inspired Prospector Saloon
- One of the few settlements not aligned with any major faction
- Victor can be found patrolling the town at night
- Sunny Smiles' dog Cheyenne helps with tutorial combat
