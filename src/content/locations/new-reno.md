## Overview

**New Reno** is a major city in post-apocalyptic Northern California, appearing in Fallout 2. Unlike other settlements that focus on survival or rebuilding, New Reno embraces viceâ€”it's a city of gambling, prostitution, drug trade, and organized crime, controlled by four warring families.

## History

### Pre-War

Reno was a gambling and entertainment city in Nevada, though smaller and seedier than its neighbor Las Vegas. When the bombs fell, the city survived relatively intact due to its distance from major targets.

### Post-War Development

Rather than rebuild as a conventional settlement, New Reno's survivors embraced the city's pre-war identity. Crime families rose to power, dividing the city among themselves and establishing a brutal but stable order.

## The Four Families

New Reno is controlled by four crime families, each with distinct territories and specialties:

### The Mordino Family

- **Territory** - Desperado Casino, Morningstar Mine
- **Boss** - Big Jesus Mordino
- **Specialty** - Jet production and distribution
- **Notable** - Created Jet, the most addictive drug in the wasteland

### The Salvatore Family

- **Territory** - Salvatore's Bar
- **Boss** - Louis Salvatore
- **Specialty** - Energy weapons, Enclave connections
- **Notable** - Smallest but technologically advanced

### The Bishop Family

- **Territory** - Shark Club Casino
- **Boss** - John Bishop
- **Specialty** - Political manipulation, NCR connections
- **Notable** - Seeks to influence the expanding NCR

### The Wright Family

- **Territory** - Wright Estate
- **Boss** - Orville Wright
- **Specialty** - Bootleg alcohol production
- **Notable** - Most honorable family, wants to go legitimate

## Jet

New Reno is infamous as the birthplace of Jet:
- Created by Myron at the Mordino Stables
- Made from brahmin dung fumes
- Extremely addictive
- Major source of Mordino wealth
- Spreads across the wasteland from here

## Made Man

The Chosen One can rise through New Reno's criminal ranks:
- Work for any of the four families
- Complete jobs (assassination, theft, sabotage)
- Become a "Made Man" in one family
- This creates enemies with rival families

### Becoming a Porn Star

Alternatively, the Chosen One can pursue a career at Golden Globes:
- Gain fame as a porn star
- Special reputation throughout New Reno
- Humorous alternative to crime

## Boxing

The Jungle gym offers boxing opportunities:
- Fight your way up the ranks
- Face famous boxers
- Bet on fights
- Become heavyweight champion

## The Dark Side

New Reno showcases Fallout's willingness to embrace mature themes:
- Prostitution is common
- Drug addiction destroys lives
- Murder is a business
- Slavery exists on the fringes
- The families are genuinely evil

## NCR Relations

New Reno exists in tension with the expanding NCR:
- The Bishop family seeks alliance with NCR politicians
- Other families resist NCR encroachment
- The city represents the chaotic alternative to NCR order
- Its future with NCR expansion is uncertain

## Legacy

New Reno represents:
- Fallout's willingness to explore moral darkness
- Player freedom to embrace evil or fight it
- The complexity of post-apocalyptic societies
- An alternative to the hero narrative
