Skills & Perks

The 18 skills of Skyrim

Skyrim has 18 skills divided into three categories: Warrior (Combat), Mage (Magic), and Thief (Stealth). Skills level up through use, and perks are unlocked with perk points gained on level up.

Leveling System

Your character level increases as your skills increase. Higher skills contribute more to leveling. Max skill level is 100, and skills can be "Legendary" reset for infinite leveling.

Warrior Skills

Smithing Warrior

Craft and improve weapons and armor. Essential for any combat build.

  • Key Perks: Steel Smithing → Dwarven → Orcish → Ebony → Daedric
  • Power Level: Craft iron daggers or leather bracers early, then gold jewelry

Heavy Armor Warrior

Reduces damage from heavy armor. Tank builds essential.

  • Key Perks: Juggernaut, Conditioning (weightless armor), Reflect Blows
  • Best Armor: Daedric, Dragonplate, Ebony

Block Warrior

Block attacks with shields or weapons.

  • Key Perks: Shield Wall, Deflect Arrows, Shield Charge

Two-Handed Warrior

Greatswords, battleaxes, and warhammers.

  • Key Perks: Barbarian, Champion's Stance, Devastating Blow

One-Handed Warrior

Swords, axes, maces, and daggers.

  • Key Perks: Armsman, Fighting Stance, Dual Savagery

Archery Warrior

Bows and crossbows. Amazing for stealth builds.

  • Key Perks: Overdraw, Eagle Eye, Steady Hand, Quick Shot

Mage Skills

Destruction Mage

Fire, frost, and shock damage spells.

  • Key Perks: Augmented Flames/Frost/Shock, Impact (stagger)
  • Best Spells: Incinerate, Thunderbolt, Blizzard

Conjuration Mage

Summon creatures and raise the dead.

  • Key Perks: Twin Souls (2 summons), Necromancy, Atromancy
  • Best Summons: Dremora Lord, Thralls

Restoration Mage

Healing and anti-undead magic.

  • Key Perks: Recovery, Avoid Death, Necromage (affects vampires)

Alteration Mage

Armor spells, paralysis, and utility magic.

  • Key Perks: Mage Armor (3x armor when unarmored), Paralysis

Illusion Mage

Calm, fear, frenzy, and invisibility.

  • Key Perks: Quiet Casting (essential for stealth mages), Master of the Mind

Enchanting Mage

Enchant weapons and armor. Extremely powerful.

  • Key Perks: Insightful Enchanter, Extra Effect (2 enchants)
  • Power Level: Disenchant everything, enchant iron daggers with Banish

Thief Skills

Sneak Thief

Move undetected and perform sneak attacks.

  • Key Perks: Backstab (6x dagger), Assassin's Blade (15x!), Shadow Warrior
  • Power Level: Sneak attack Hadvar/Ralof at Helgen

Light Armor Thief

Mobility-focused armor.

  • Key Perks: Agile Defender, Unhindered, Deft Movement
  • Best Armor: Dragonscale, Glass, Deathbrand (unique)

Lockpicking Thief

Open locked chests and doors.

  • Key Perks: Unbreakable (picks don't break), Locksmith

Pickpocket Thief

Steal items from NPCs.

  • Key Perks: Perfect Touch (steal equipped items), Poisoned

Speech Thief

Better prices and persuasion.

  • Key Perks: Merchant (sell anything to anyone), Fence, Master Trader

Alchemy Thief

Create potions and poisons. Incredibly powerful.

  • Key Perks: Benefactor, Poisoner, Purity
  • Best Potions: Fortify Smithing, Fortify Enchanting, Invisibility
Legendary Skills

Once a skill reaches 100, you can make it "Legendary" to reset it to 15 and refund all perks. This allows unlimited character leveling but you lose all perks in that skill temporarily.
