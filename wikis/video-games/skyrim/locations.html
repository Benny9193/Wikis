Locations

Major cities, dungeons, and points of interest

Skyrim is a vast province with diverse landscapes, from frozen tundra to volcanic hot springs. This page catalogs the major locations you'll encounter.

The Nine Holds

Whiterun Hold

Capital: Whiterun | Jarl: Balgruuf the Greater

Central plains of Skyrim, home to Dragonsreach and the Companions. The most accessible hold for new players.

  • Dragonsreach - Jarl's palace, ancient dragon trap
  • Jorrvaskr - Companions headquarters
  • Gildergreen - Sacred tree in the Wind District
  • Western Watchtower - First dragon encounter

Haafingar

Capital: Solitude | Jarl: Elisif the Fair

Northwestern hold, seat of Imperial power in Skyrim. Home to the Bards College.

  • Blue Palace - Seat of the High King
  • Castle Dour - Imperial military headquarters
  • Bards College - Musical education
  • Thalmor Embassy - Infiltration target

Eastmarch

Capital: Windhelm | Jarl: Ulfric Stormcloak

Eastern volcanic region, Stormcloak rebellion headquarters.

  • Palace of the Kings - Ulfric's seat
  • Gray Quarter - Dunmer refugee district
  • Kynesgrove - Dragon resurrection site

The Reach

Capital: Markarth | Jarl: Igmund

Western mountains, built in ancient Dwemer ruins. Forsworn territory.

  • Understone Keep - Dwemer architecture
  • Cidhna Mine - Prison mine
  • Nchuand-Zel - Dwemer excavation

The Rift

Capital: Riften | Jarl: Laila Law-Giver

Southeastern forests, home to the Thieves Guild.

  • The Ratway - Thieves Guild location
  • Goldenglow Estate - Guild quest target
  • Honningbrew Meadery - Competition elimination
