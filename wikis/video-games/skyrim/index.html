About This Wiki

This is a personal Skyrim wiki for tracking quests, characters, locations, and lore. Use it to document your journey through the frozen province of Skyrim as the legendary Dragonborn.

The Story

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is set 200 years after the events of Oblivion, in the northern province of Skyrim. The player takes on the role of the Dragonborn, a mortal with the soul of a dragon, destined to defeat Alduin the World-Eater and save Tamriel from destruction.

Setting

Location: Skyrim, northernmost province of Tamriel
Era: Fourth Era, Year 201
Conflict: Civil War between Imperials and Stormcloaks, Dragon Crisis

The Nine Holds

Skyrim is divided into nine holds, each with its own capital city and Jarl:

  • Whiterun Hold - Whiterun (central hub)
  • Haafingar - Solitude (Imperial capital)
  • Eastmarch - Windhelm (Stormcloak capital)
  • The Reach - Markarth (Dwemer ruins)
  • The Rift - Riften (Thieves Guild)
  • Falkreath Hold - Falkreath
  • Hjaalmarch - Morthal
  • The Pale - Dawnstar
  • Winterhold - Winterhold (College)
