Main Quest

The path of the Dragonborn

The main questline follows the Dragonborn's journey to defeat Alduin, the World-Eater. The quest begins with the player's execution at Helgen and ends with a confrontation in Sovngarde.

Point of No Return

After "The World-Eater's Eyrie," you cannot return to Skyrim until completing the main quest. Make sure you're prepared!

Key Rewards

  • Unrelenting Force - First shout, learned early
  • Whirlwind Sprint - From the Greybeards
  • Dragonrend - Essential for fighting Alduin
  • Clear Skies - Required for Throat of the World
  • Call of Valor - Summon heroes from Sovngarde
