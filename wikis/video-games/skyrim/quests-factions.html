Faction Quests

Join the guilds and shape your destiny

Skyrim features four major factions, each with their own questline, rewards, and consequences. You can complete all faction questlines on a single character.

The Companions

Jorrvaskr, Whiterun • Warriors Guild • Werewolf Transformation

The Companions are Skyrim's premier warriors guild, descended from the Five Hundred Companions of Ysgramor. Their inner circle, The Circle, shares the secret of lycanthropy.

Joining

Go to Jorrvaskr in Whiterun and speak to Kodlak Whitemane. Fight Vilkas in the courtyard to prove your worth.

Thieves Guild

The Ratway, Riften • Criminal Organization • Nightingale Powers

The Thieves Guild has fallen on hard times, suffering from a curse that brings bad luck to all their endeavors. Help restore the Guild to its former glory and uncover the source of their misfortune.

Joining

Speak to Brynjolf in Riften's marketplace. Complete his "A Chance Arrangement" pickpocket job to get recruited.

Skeleton Key

The Skeleton Key is an unbreakable lockpick. You can keep it indefinitely before completing "Darkness Returns," but this locks you out of the Nightingale powers.

Dark Brotherhood

Dawnstar Sanctuary • Assassins • Spectral Assassin

The Dark Brotherhood is a family of assassins who worship Sithis and follow the commands of the Night Mother. Their sanctuary has seen better days, but a new Listener may change their fate.

Joining

Kill Grelod the Kind in Riften's orphanage (quest "Innocence Lost" from Aventus Aretino in Windhelm). Sleep anywhere to be abducted by Astrid.

College of Winterhold

Winterhold • Mages Guild • Archmage Robes

The College of Winterhold is Skyrim's center of magical learning, isolated on a cliff after the Great Collapse destroyed most of the city. Uncover the secrets of the Eye of Magnus and the Psijic Order.

Joining

Speak to Faralda at the bridge to Winterhold. Demonstrate a spell (she'll teach you one if needed) to gain entry.
