Characters

NPCs, followers, and key figures of Skyrim

Skyrim is populated with hundreds of unique characters. This page covers the most important NPCs, potential followers, and marriage candidates.

Legend

Follower Can join you as a companion
Marriageable Can be married with Amulet of Mara
Essential Cannot be killed
Trainer Can train skills
Merchant Buys/sells goods

Followers - Warriors

Lydia

Dragonsreach / Breezehome, Whiterun
Follower Marriageable

Your first housecarl, assigned after becoming Thane of Whiterun. Heavy armor and one-handed specialist. The iconic "I am sworn to carry your burdens" companion.

Uthgerd the Unbroken

The Bannered Mare, Whiterun
Follower Marriageable

Brawl her to earn her respect. Two-handed warrior who was rejected from the Companions for killing a boy during training.

Mjoll the Lioness

Riften
Follower Marriageable Essential

Adventurer who lost her sword Grimsever in a Dwemer ruin. Retrieve it to recruit her. Cannot die, making her one of the best followers.

Vilkas

Jorrvaskr, Whiterun
Follower Marriageable Trainer

Member of the Circle. Master Two-Handed trainer. Available after completing the Companions questline.

Farkas

Jorrvaskr, Whiterun
Follower Marriageable Trainer

Vilkas's twin brother. Heavy Armor trainer. Your shield-brother during the Companions initiation.

Aela the Huntress

Jorrvaskr, Whiterun
Follower Marriageable Trainer

Expert archer and werewolf. Archery trainer. Can restore your lycanthropy even after being cured.

Followers - Mages

J'zargo

College of Winterhold
Follower

Ambitious Khajiit mage. Complete his flame cloak scroll quest to recruit. One of the few followers with no level cap.

Marcurio

Bee and Barb, Riften
Follower Marriageable

Mercenary mage for hire (500 gold). Destruction specialist with witty dialogue. "I'm an apprentice wizard, not a pack mule!"

Serana

Dimhollow Crypt (Dawnguard DLC)
Follower Essential

Ancient vampire and daughter of Lord Harkon. One of the most developed companions with unique dialogue. Can turn you into a vampire.

Teldryn Sero

Retching Netch, Raven Rock (Dragonborn DLC)
Follower

Dunmer spellsword mercenary (500 gold). Combines destruction magic with one-handed combat. Great dialogue.

Followers - Stealth

Cicero

Dawnstar Sanctuary
Follower Essential

The Keeper of the Night Mother. Insane jester assassin. Spare him in the Dark Brotherhood questline to recruit.

Jenassa

The Drunken Huntsman, Whiterun
Follower Marriageable

Dunmer mercenary (500 gold). Archery and light armor specialist. "There is no hunt like the hunt for men."

Key Story Characters

Paarthurnax

Throat of the World
Essential

Ancient dragon and leader of the Greybeards. Brother of Alduin who turned against him. The Blades want him dead for his past crimes. "What is better - to be born good, or to overcome your evil nature through great effort?"

Delphine

Sleeping Giant Inn, Riverwood → Sky Haven Temple
Essential

Last of the Blades, dragon hunters who served the Dragonborn emperors. Paranoid but competent. Demands Paarthurnax's death.

Esbern

The Ratway, Riften → Sky Haven Temple
Essential

Blades loremaster hiding from the Thalmor. Expert on dragons and the prophecy of Alduin's return.

Arngeir

High Hrothgar
Essential

Spokesperson for the Greybeards. Teaches the Dragonborn the Way of the Voice.

Marriage Candidates

To marry, obtain the Amulet of Mara from Maramal in Riften (200 gold), wear it, and speak to eligible NPCs.

Best Spouses

Mjoll the Lioness - Essential, powerful follower
Aela the Huntress - Can restore werewolf blood, trainer
Marcurio - Strong mage, good income from store
Ysolda - Runs profitable store (Whiterun)
