Crafting

Smithing, Alchemy, and Enchanting

The three crafting skills form the backbone of character power in Skyrim. Combined, they create a feedback loop that can produce gear far beyond anything found in the world.

The Crafting Loop (Resto-Loop)

1 Create Fortify Alchemy gear with Enchanting
2 Wear gear, make Fortify Enchanting potions
3 Drink potion, create stronger Fortify Alchemy gear
4 Repeat until satisfied, then make Fortify Smithing potions
5 Wear Fortify Smithing gear + drink potion, improve weapons/armor

Smithing

Forge weapons and armor, then improve them at a grindstone or workbench

Smithing Perk Paths

100% Cost Reduction

With Extra Effect perk and maxed Enchanting, you can achieve 100% spell cost reduction for any magic school:

  • Head: Fortify [School] + Fortify [School 2] = -50%
  • Chest: Fortify [School] + Fortify [School 2] = -50%
  • Ring: Fortify [School] + Fortify [School 2]
  • Necklace: Fortify [School] + Fortify [School 2]
Enchanting Tips

• Use Grand or Black Soul Gems for maximum power
• Azura's Star/Black Star = infinite reusable soul gem
• Disenchant everything new to learn effects
• Fortify Alchemy only goes on Head, Gloves, Ring, Necklace

Power Leveling Enchanting

  1. Gather iron daggers or leather bracers
  2. Fill soul gems (Soul Trap or bound weapons)
  3. Enchant with Banish (highest XP) or any effect
  4. Sell for profit (Banish daggers sell for 1000+ gold)
