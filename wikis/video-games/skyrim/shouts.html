Dragon Shouts

Thu'um - The Voice

Dragon Shouts (Thu'um) are powerful abilities unique to the Dragonborn. Each shout consists of three Words of Power learned from Word Walls scattered throughout Skyrim. Dragon souls are required to unlock words.

How Shouts Work

Find Word Walls to learn words, then spend dragon souls to unlock them. More words = stronger effect but longer cooldown.

Essential Shouts

Unrelenting Force

Fus Ro Dah
FUS RO DAH

The iconic shout. Pushes enemies away with tremendous force. At full power, sends enemies flying. First shout you learn.

Dragonrend

Joor Zah Frul
JOOR ZAH FRUL

Forces dragons to land. Essential for the main quest. Learned from the Elder Scroll vision.

Slow Time

Tiid Klo Ul
TIID KLO UL

Slows time significantly while you move at normal speed. Devastating with archery or melee.

Become Ethereal

Feim Zii Gron
FEIM ZII GRON

Become invulnerable but cannot attack. Perfect for escaping danger or surviving long falls.

Combat Shouts

Fire Breath

Yol Toor Shul
YOL TOOR SHUL

Breathe fire like a dragon. Good damage, sets enemies on fire.

Frost Breath

Fo Krah Diin
FO KRAH DIIN

Breathe frost, dealing cold damage and draining stamina.

Marked for Death

Krii Lun Aus
KRII LUN AUS

Permanently reduces enemy armor and drains health over time. Stacks!

Utility Shouts

Whirlwind Sprint

Wuld Nah Kest
WULD NAH KEST

Dash forward at incredible speed. Great for traversal and escaping.

Clear Skies

Lok Vah Koor
LOK VAH KOOR

Clears weather. Required to reach the Throat of the World.

Aura Whisper

Laas Yah Nir
LAAS YAH NIR

Detect all life nearby through walls. Essential for stealth builds.

Throw Voice

Zul Mey Gut
ZUL MEY GUT

Throws your voice to distract enemies. "Hey, melon nose!"
