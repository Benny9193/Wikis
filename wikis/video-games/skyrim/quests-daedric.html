Daedric Quests

Serve the Daedric Princes for powerful artifacts

The Daedric Princes are god-like beings from Oblivion. Each has a quest in Skyrim that rewards a unique Daedric Artifact. Collecting 15 artifacts unlocks the "Oblivion Walker" achievement.

Achievement Note

For "Oblivion Walker," you need 15 artifacts. The Skeleton Key doesn't count, and some quests have choices that affect which artifact you receive. See individual quests for details.

The Daedric Princes

Azura No Level Req

Prince of Dusk and Dawn, the Magic Between Day and Night
Quest: The Black Star
Start: Speak to Aranea Ienith at the Shrine of Azura (south of Winterhold)
Status: Not Started

Find the broken Azura's Star and choose whether to cleanse it for Azura or corrupt it for Nelacar.

Azura's Star / The Black Star
Reusable soul gem. Azura's Star captures white souls only. The Black Star captures all souls (better choice).

Boethiah Level 30+

Prince of Deceit, Conspiracy, and Secret Plots of Murder
Quest: Boethiah's Calling
Start: Read "Boethiah's Proving" or find Sacellum of Boethiah (east of Windhelm)
Status: Not Started

Sacrifice a follower on Boethiah's altar, then kill the other cultists. Slay the current champion at Knifepoint Ridge.

Ebony Mail
Heavy armor. Muffle effect, poisons nearby enemies (5 damage/sec).

Clavicus Vile Level 10+

Prince of Power, Trickery, Wishes, and Bargains
Quest: A Daedra's Best Friend
Start: Speak to Barbas (dog) outside Falkreath
Status: Not Started

Reunite Barbas with Clavicus Vile. Choose to kill Barbas for the Rueful Axe or refuse for the Masque.

Masque of Clavicus Vile (Keep Barbas alive)
+10 Speech, +10% better prices, Regenerate Magicka 5%. THIS is the artifact for the achievement!

Hermaeus Mora Level 15+

Prince of Knowledge, Memory, and Fate
Quest: Discerning the Transmundane
Start: Septimus Signus's Outpost (north of Winterhold)
Status: Not Started

Help Septimus open a Dwemer lockbox using blood from all mer races (Altmer, Bosmer, Dunmer, Falmer, Orsimer).

Oghma Infinium
Book that grants +5 to all skills in a path (Combat, Magic, or Stealth). One-time use.

Hircine No Level Req

Prince of the Hunt, Sport, and the Great Game
Quest: Ill Met by Moonlight
Start: Speak to Sinding in Falkreath Jail
Status: Not Started

Hunt Sinding the werewolf. Choose to kill him for the Savior's Hide or help him for the Ring of Hircine.

Savior's Hide / Ring of Hircine
Hide: +15% Magic Resistance, +50% Poison Resistance. Ring: Extra werewolf transformation per day. Both count as artifacts!

Malacath No Level Req

Prince of the Spurned, Ostracized, and Bloody Oath
Quest: The Cursed Tribe
Start: Largashbur (Orc stronghold, The Rift)
Status: Not Started

Help the Orcs lift a curse by bringing Troll Fat and a Daedra Heart, then defeat the giant Yamarz was supposed to kill.

Volendrung
Warhammer. Absorb 50 stamina per hit. Excellent for power attack builds.

Mehrunes Dagon Level 20+

Prince of Destruction, Change, Revolution, and Ambition
Quest: Pieces of the Past
Start: Visit Silus Vesuius's museum in Dawnstar
Status: Not Started

Collect the pieces of Mehrunes' Razor. At the shrine, kill Silus to receive the reforged dagger.

Mehrunes' Razor
Dagger. 1.98% chance to instantly kill any enemy. Works on dragons!

Mephala Level 20+

Prince of Lies, Secrets, and Plots
Quest: The Whispering Door
Start: Ask innkeeper about Jarl Balgruuf's children (Whiterun)
Status: Not Started

Investigate Nelkir's strange behavior and obtain the key to the Whispering Door in Dragonsreach basement.

Ebony Blade
Katana. Absorb 10-30 health (powered up by killing friends). Infinite charge, fast swing.

Meridia Level 12+

Prince of Life, Light, and the Energies of Living Things
Quest: The Break of Dawn
Start: Find Meridia's Beacon (random loot) or visit her statue (west of Solitude)
Status: Not Started

Cleanse Meridia's temple of the necromancer Malkoran and his shades.

Dawnbreaker
Sword. Burns for 10-25 fire damage. Chance to cause undead explosion. Amazing vs. draugr.

Molag Bal No Level Req

Prince of Domination, Enslavement, and Vampirism
Quest: The House of Horrors
Start: Speak to Vigilant Tyranus in Markarth
Status: Not Started

Investigate the abandoned house, become trapped, and beat a priest into submission for Molag Bal.

Mace of Molag Bal
Mace. Absorb 25 magicka and stamina, soul trap for 3 seconds.

Namira No Level Req

Prince of the Ancient Darkness, Decay, and Cannibalism
Quest: The Taste of Death
Start: Speak to Eola in the Markarth Hall of the Dead
Status: Not Started

Clear Reachcliff Cave and lure Brother Verulus to be sacrificed in a cannibalistic feast.

Ring of Namira
+50 Stamina. Feeding on corpses restores 50 health and boosts health/stamina regen.

Nocturnal No Level Req

Prince of Night, Darkness, Luck, and the Unknown
Quest: Thieves Guild Questline
Start: Complete the Thieves Guild (see Faction Quests)
Status: Not Started

Become a Nightingale and receive Nocturnal's blessing.

Skeleton Key (doesn't count for achievement)
Unbreakable lockpick. Must return it to unlock Nightingale powers.

Peryite Level 12+

Prince of Pestilence, Tasks, and Natural Order
Quest: The Only Cure
Start: Shrine of Peryite (The Reach, requires inhaling incense)
Status: Not Started

Kill the Afflicted's leader Orchendor in Bthardamz to end the plague.

Spellbreaker
Shield. Creates a ward blocking 50 spell damage while blocking.

Sanguine Level 14+

Prince of Debauchery, Hedonism, and Indulgence
Quest: A Night to Remember
Start: Meet Sam Guevenne at any tavern, win drinking contest
Status: Not Started

Piece together a wild night of drunken adventures across Skyrim. One of the most entertaining quests!

Sanguine Rose
Staff. Summons a Dremora to fight for 60 seconds.

Sheogorath No Level Req

Prince of Madness, Creativity, and the Unpredictable
Quest: The Mind of Madness
Start: Speak to Dervenin in Solitude (near Bards College)
Status: Not Started

Enter the mind of Pelagius the Mad and help Sheogorath escape by "treating" his insanity.

Wabbajack
Staff. Random transformation effects - can turn enemies into chickens, sweetrolls, or Dremora Lords!

Vaermina No Level Req

Prince of Dreams, Nightmares, and Evil Omens
Quest: Waking Nightmare
Start: Speak to Erandur in Dawnstar's inn about nightmares
Status: Not Started

Enter Nightcaller Temple to destroy the Skull of Corruption. Vaermina asks you to kill Erandur - obey her for the artifact!

Skull of Corruption (must kill Erandur)
Staff. 20 damage, or 50 if charged by harvesting dreams from sleeping NPCs.
