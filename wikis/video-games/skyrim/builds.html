Character Builds

Optimized playstyles for every type of adventurer

Skyrim allows complete freedom in character building. These are proven builds that excel at their roles, with skill priorities and gear recommendations.

Build Philosophy

Primary Skills: Focus your perks here first
Secondary Skills: Support skills, level naturally or with spare perks
Difficulty: How challenging to play effectively

Stealth Builds

Stealth Archer

The build everyone becomes eventually

Primary Skills

Archery Sneak Light Armor

Secondary Skills

Alchemy Smithing Enchanting

Key Perks

  • Sneak: Stealth 5/5, Backstab, Deadly Aim (3x bow damage), Shadow Warrior
  • Archery: Overdraw 5/5, Eagle Eye, Steady Hand, Quick Shot
  • Light Armor: Agile Defender 5/5, Custom Fit

Recommended Gear

Armor: Dragonscale or Guild Master
Weapon: Dragonbone Bow
Enchants: Fortify Archery, Sneak
Arrows: Daedric or Dragonbone

Difficulty

Easy - Extremely overpowered
Playstyle: Crouch, headshot, repeat. The 3x sneak attack bonus with bows makes almost everything a one-shot. Use Aura Whisper to track enemies through walls.

Assassin

Silent killer with daggers

Primary Skills

Sneak One-Handed Alchemy

Secondary Skills

Light Armor Illusion Pickpocket

Key Perks

  • Sneak: Backstab, Assassin's Blade (15x dagger damage!), Silence, Shadow Warrior
  • One-Handed: Armsman 5/5, Dual Flurry, Dual Savagery
  • Alchemy: Poisoner, Concentrated Poison
  • Illusion: Quiet Casting (essential!)

Recommended Gear

Armor: Shrouded/Ancient Shrouded
Weapon: Blade of Woe, Mehrunes' Razor
Ring: Muffle enchant
Poisons: Paralysis, Damage Health

Difficulty

Easy-Medium - Position dependent
Playstyle: 15x sneak attack damage with daggers is absurd. Use Invisibility potions or spells to reposition. Mehrunes' Razor adds instant-kill chance. Dark Brotherhood questline is mandatory.

Warrior Builds

Two-Handed Berserker

Devastating power attacks

Primary Skills

Two-Handed Heavy Armor Smithing

Secondary Skills

Enchanting Block Alchemy

Key Perks

  • Two-Handed: Barbarian 5/5, Champion's Stance, Devastating Blow, Sweep
  • Heavy Armor: Juggernaut 5/5, Conditioning (weightless armor)
  • Smithing: Daedric or Dragonbone path

Recommended Gear

Armor: Daedric or Dragonplate
Weapon: Volendrung or Daedric Greatsword
Enchants: Fortify Two-Handed, Stamina Regen
Shout: Elemental Fury (doesn't work on enchanted weapons)

Difficulty

Easy-Medium - Simple and effective
Playstyle: Power attack everything. Sweep hits multiple enemies. Volendrung's stamina absorption means infinite power attacks. Use Vegetable Soup for continuous stamina.

Sword and Shield Tank

Immovable object

Primary Skills

One-Handed Block Heavy Armor

Secondary Skills

Smithing Restoration Enchanting

Key Perks

  • Block: Shield Wall 5/5, Deflect Arrows, Elemental Protection, Shield Charge
  • One-Handed: Armsman 5/5, Fighting Stance, Savage Strike
  • Heavy Armor: Juggernaut 5/5, Fists of Steel, Conditioning, Reflect Blows

Recommended Gear

Armor: Daedric with Shield
Shield: Spellbreaker or Auriel's Shield
Weapon: Mace of Molag Bal, Dawnbreaker
Enchants: Fortify Block, Magic Resist

Difficulty

Very Easy - Nearly unkillable
Playstyle: Block everything, bash to interrupt, counterattack. Shield Charge turns you into a bowling ball. With Spellbreaker, even dragons can't hurt you.

Mage Builds

Pure Destruction Mage

Master of the elements

Primary Skills

Destruction Enchanting Restoration

Secondary Skills

Alteration Conjuration Alchemy

Key Perks

  • Destruction: All Augmented perks, Impact (stagger), Dual Casting
  • Enchanting: Extra Effect for stacking cost reduction
  • Alteration: Mage Armor 3/3 (if not wearing armor)

Recommended Gear

Armor: Archmage's Robes or enchanted robes
Head: Morokei (Dragon Priest mask)
Enchants: Fortify Destruction + Magicka Regen
Goal: 100% Destruction cost reduction

Difficulty

Medium - Needs gear to shine
Playstyle: Impact-stagger enemies with dual-cast spells. They never get to attack. Once you hit 100% cost reduction, you have infinite spells. Use Slow Time shout for crowd control.

Necromancer

Army of the dead

Primary Skills

Conjuration Destruction Enchanting

Secondary Skills

Alteration Illusion Restoration

Key Perks

  • Conjuration: Necromancy, Dark Souls, Twin Souls (2 minions!)
  • Destruction: Augmented Frost (thematic)
  • Restoration: Necromage (boosts everything if vampire)

Recommended Gear

Armor: Vampire Royal Armor or Black Robes
Weapon: Staff of Reanimation
Ring: Ring of Necromancy (from Dawnguard)
Race: Dark Elf or Vampire

Difficulty

Easy-Medium - Minions do the work
Playstyle: Raise the strongest enemies you kill to fight for you. Twin Souls lets you have 2 permanent thralls. Vampirism + Necromage boosts all your abilities by 25%.

Hybrid Builds

Battlemage

Spell in one hand, sword in the other

Primary Skills

One-Handed Destruction Heavy Armor

Secondary Skills

Conjuration Restoration Enchanting

Key Perks

  • One-Handed: Armsman 5/5, Fighting Stance
  • Destruction: Augmented element of choice, Impact (one-handed casting still staggers)
  • Heavy Armor: Juggernaut, Conditioning

Recommended Gear

Armor: Ebony Mail or Daedric
Weapon: Chillrend or Dragonbone Sword
Enchants: Fortify Destruction, One-Handed
Spell: Flames â†’ Incinerate

Difficulty

Medium - Versatile but splits focus
Playstyle: Engage with destruction magic, close to melee range, finish with sword. Very flexible - use flames to whittle while backing up, or fireball groups before charging in.

Spellsword

Bound weapons and light armor agility

Primary Skills

Conjuration One-Handed Light Armor

Secondary Skills

Alteration Restoration Enchanting

Key Perks

  • Conjuration: Mystic Binding (stronger bound weapons), Soul Stealer, Oblivion Binding
  • One-Handed: Armsman 5/5, Dual Flurry
  • Alteration: Magic Resistance perks

Recommended Gear

Armor: Nightingale or Glass
Weapons: Bound Sword (auto soul trap)
Enchants: Fortify Conjuration, One-Handed
Standing Stone: Atronach (absorb spells)

Difficulty

Easy-Medium - Bound weapons are free!
Playstyle: Summon bound sword/bow as needed - they're weightless and scale with perks. Soul Stealer means free soul gems. Dual wield bound swords for maximum DPS.
