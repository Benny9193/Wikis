How to Use This Wiki

Organize your notes by topic or project. Use this wiki as your second brain for:

  • Quick references - Code snippets, commands, and shortcuts

  • Research - Collected information on topics you're exploring
  • Ideas - Thoughts and concepts to develop later
Note

The best notes are the ones you actually use. Keep them searchable and well-organized.

Note-Taking Tips

  1. Write for your future self - Include context you might forget
  2. Use examples - Concrete examples are easier to understand than abstract descriptions
  3. Link related notes - Build connections between ideas
  4. Review regularly - Spaced repetition helps retention
